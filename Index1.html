  • Bhagavad Gita As It Is by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada is a translation and commentary on the Bhagavad Gita from the Gaudiya Vaishnavism perspective. It emphasizes devotion (bhakti) to Krishna, explaining duty (dharma), selfless action (karma yoga), and spiritual wisdom (jnana yoga).
